Who We Are

Built on trust,
driven by precision.

Danley Logistics Ltd was founded in 2012 with a single truck and a commitment to doing things differently. Built on reliability, transparency, and a deep understanding of the East African trade corridor, we have grown into a trusted integrated logistics partner for businesses across Kenya and the wider region.

From our Nairobi headquarters, Mombasa port operations, and Denmark European hub, we manage every link in the supply chain. In 2026 we launched our second division, The Euro-Archive by Danley — bringing premium European fashion directly to Nairobi buyers at prices that make sense.

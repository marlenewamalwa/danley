Our Story

From one truck
to a corridor.

Danley Logistics Ltd was founded in Nairobi in 2012 with a single truck, a small team, and a clear vision: to build a logistics company that East African businesses could genuinely trust. No hidden fees. No missed windows. No excuses.

In the years that followed, we expanded our fleet and our footprint — adding bonded warehousing in Nairobi, a port operations office in Mombasa, and establishing our European hub in Denmark to manage the growing demand for cross-continental freight.

Today, Danley Logistics is a fully integrated logistics partner operating across road, air, and sea. We handle everything from customs clearance at the Port of Mombasa to last-mile delivery in Nairobi's inner suburbs. Our institutional knowledge of KRA processes and East African trade lanes is a competitive advantage our clients rely on every day.

In 2026, we activated our second growth engine — The Euro-Archive by Danley — a fashion retail division that leverages our Denmark hub and logistics infrastructure to bring authenticated European overstock directly to Kenyan buyers at prices that make the market sit up and take notice.

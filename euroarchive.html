The Concept

The supply chain
is the advantage.

The Euro-Archive is Danley's fashion retail division — a 2026 launch that turns our Denmark hub and logistics infrastructure into a retail competitive advantage. We source authenticated overstock and returns from the Zalando network in Germany, ship it through our Denmark office, clear it through our own customs team in Kenya, and sell it to Nairobi buyers at a fraction of what the same item costs in a Westgate mall.

No third-party freight broker. No middleman importer. No inflated landing costs. The margin structure that The Euro-Archive operates on is only possible because Danley owns the entire supply chain from European warehouse to Nairobi storefront.

The Process

How your stock
gets here.

01
Source in Europe

We tap the Zalando overstock and returns network in Germany — authenticated, graded, and ready for re-sale. Our Denmark team selects and negotiates on every batch.

02
Ship via Denmark Hub

Stock is consolidated at our Denmark office and shipped to Kenya via our own freight forwarding operation — no third-party brokers, no broker markups on the lane.

03
Clear Customs In-House

Our KRA-licensed customs agents handle the full clearance — IDF, duty assessment, CoC handling — so the cargo clears fast and the duty cost is exact. No surprises.

04
Sell in Nairobi

Stock lands in our Nairobi warehouse, priced at 60–75% below what the same pieces cost in Westgate or Garden City — and sells fast because the quality is authenticated.

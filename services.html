Service 01

Dedicated
Transport

Our dedicated road transport service covers Kenya and the wider East African region. We operate a maintained fleet of trucks — from light rigids for urban delivery to heavy articulated units for long-haul freight — all managed under a single dispatch team that knows your route, your cargo, and your timelines.

Full-load (FTL) and groupage (LTL) options are available. Clients get a single point of contact, real-time cargo updates, and delivery confirmation. No third-party surprises. No broker markups.

Service 02

Warehousing &
Distribution

Danley operates bonded and general warehousing facilities in both Nairobi and Mombasa, giving clients flexible storage options at the two most critical points in the Kenyan supply chain.

Our facilities are managed with a full inventory tracking system. Clients have visibility of their stock levels in real time. We offer pick-and-pack services, container devanning, palletisation, and outbound distribution — making our warehouses a true hub in your supply chain, not just a holding room.

Service 03

Freight
Forwarding

Air and sea freight forwarding for imports and exports. We handle bookings, documentation, consolidation, and coordination with carriers on your behalf — so you don't have to learn a new language every time you move cargo internationally.

Our Denmark hub gives us a direct lane for Europe-to-Kenya shipments that saves clients both time and money. For sea freight, we work through the Port of Mombasa. For air, JKIA is our gateway with multiple airline relationships giving you competitive rates.

Service 04

Customs
Clearance

KRA customs clearance is where cargo either moves or gets stuck. Our licensed customs agents have handled hundreds of entries across Mombasa port, JKIA, and inland container depots — and they know exactly how to move your cargo without unnecessary delays.

We manage all documentation including import declarations, certificates of conformity, duty assessments, and release orders. We stay current on KRA regulatory changes so our clients never get caught out by a rule that changed last month.

Last-Mile
Delivery

The last mile is where the client experience is made or broken. Our last-mile delivery operation covers Nairobi's CBD, estates, industrial areas, and surrounding peri-urban zones — with scheduled routes and on-demand options for time-sensitive deliveries.
